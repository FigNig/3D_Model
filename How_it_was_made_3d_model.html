The 3D Model

When I was making the model I took inspiration and help from Youtuber Blender guru



first I Started off with to make the chain of my 3d model so I used one of the in-blender models called torus as my base


Next I went into edit mode, grabbed the top half and moved it up to make the starting link in the chain


exiting out of edit mode I duplicated the object, move it upwards and roatate it 90 degres. using ctrl R I can repeat the actions ive just did and make a long chain


Next added in another model called a sphere and attacted that to the end of the chain to form my wrecking ball


Next I made the objects for the wrecking ball to collide into. i got a new model of a square and duplicate that object with little spaces inbetweem them. i keep duplicating it on the X, Z and Y axis until it made a giant square made of little sqaures


After I smoothed everything out, made sure the physics in the blender worked fine and fixed little bugs
